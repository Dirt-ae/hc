Score Areas

Each area is rated from 1 to 10. Final score can be weighted between jury and community votes.

Concept How clearly the edit communicates an idea, story, emotion, or direction.
Individuality How distinct the work feels compared with everyone else.
Style How well the chosen style is applied and supported throughout the edit.
Execution Timing, polish, transitions, pacing, audio choices, and technical finish.
Overall The total impact after watching it as one complete piece.
